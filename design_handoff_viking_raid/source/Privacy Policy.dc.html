RO! — VIKING RAID

PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 2026 · LAKS Studio
THE SHORT VERSION
RO! collects no directly-identifying personal data. To power the World leaderboard, your chosen player name, country and best scores are stored against an anonymous device ID. There are no accounts, no ads, and no tracking beyond that leaderboard.
1. DATA WE COLLECT
To show you on the World leaderboard, we store: the display name you choose (≤16 characters, not required to be a real name), the country you pick, your best score per stage, best combo and best clear time, and an anonymous device ID (no email, phone number, or contact info is ever collected). If you never open the World scoreboard tab, nothing is sent off your device.
2. LOCAL STORAGE
Player profiles, scores, unlocked stages and sound settings are saved in your device's local app storage so your progress survives between sessions. This local copy never leaves your device on its own — only the leaderboard fields listed in Section 1 are synced, and only for the profile you're actively playing.
3. SCOREBOARD
The in-game scoreboard offers a device view (profiles created on this device, never uploaded) and a world view (public rankings built from the data in Section 1, submitted by all players). World rankings are public by design — anyone can see the name, country and scores you've chosen to submit by playing.
4. THIRD PARTIES
The World leaderboard is hosted on Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore + Cloud Functions), which processes and stores the data described in Section 1 on our behalf under Google's infrastructure terms. The game contains no advertising networks, social plugins, or analytics/tracking SDKs of any kind.
5. DEVICE PERMISSIONS
The only device capability used is vibration, for gameplay haptic feedback. You can silence it together with sound using the in-game mute button.
6. CHILDREN
The game is suitable for all ages. It contains no chat, no user-generated content sharing, no purchases, and collects no data from anyone, including children.
7. CHANGES & CONTACT
If a future update ever changes how the game handles data, this policy will be updated first and the change will be listed here. Questions: privacy@vikingraid.example — replace with your real support email before publishing, and host this page at a public URL before submitting to the app stores.
